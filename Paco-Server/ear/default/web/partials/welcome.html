What is Easy health and what can it do?

A Platform for Rapid Prototyping of Connected Health Applications

This a prototype for behavioural and clinical studies using Experience Sampling method or Ecological Momentary Assessment. //INSERT MORE COPY HERE!!! Easy Health provides a platform with building blocks for behavioral theories. With it, you can sample the world, reason about it, and even develop interventions aimed at changing behavior. This is no small undertaking, and may never be fully accomplished, but by making it open source, working with researchers, and creating a place where participants and researchers can find each other, we hope we will make better progress and move our understanding of our behavior forward and maybe get to live better, healthier lives. This platform is an adaptation of the Open Source system PACO
