Welcome to Easy Health - A Platform for Rapid Prototyping of Connected Health Applications

Easy Health allows researchers to create and participate in behavioral science studies with groups of other people to move science forward.

Here are some tips to get started:

1) Press 'Find Experiments' to find existing experiments. Try some out.

2) Press 'Current Experiments' to manage the experiments you have joined.

This is an early beta version, but we hope you like it and please tell us about your experiences and how you are using it.

We wish you well.
